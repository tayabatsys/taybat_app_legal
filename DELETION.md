# Data Deletion Policy — AI-Taybat-App

**Contact:** tayabat.sys@gmail.com

You have the right to delete your AI-Taybat-App account and the personal data associated with it at any time. This page explains how.

## How to Request Deletion

Send an email to **tayabat.sys@gmail.com** from the email address linked to your account.

**Subject line example:**

```
Delete My Account - your.email@example.com
```

If you signed in with Facebook or Google, mention which provider you used so we can locate your account quickly. No reason or justification is required.

## What Will Be Deleted

Once your request is processed, we permanently delete:

- Your **user profile** (name, email, gender, birth date, weight, height, activity level)
- Your **health and dietary data** (health goals, conditions, dietary restrictions)
- Your **meal history and preferences** (logged meals, favorites, weekly ratings)
- Your **community content** (posts, reactions, follows)
- Your **authentication records**, including links to Facebook or Google sign-in

## What We Retain

A small amount of data may be kept where we have a legitimate or legal need:

- **Security logs** (kept for a limited period to detect abuse and protect other users)
- **Anonymized analytics** that can no longer be linked to you in any way

## Response Time

We confirm and complete deletion requests **within 30 days** of receiving your email.

## Confirmation Process

1. We may reply to verify that the request really comes from the account owner.
2. Once verified, your data is removed from our production database (Supabase).
3. You receive a final confirmation email when deletion is complete.

## Important — Deletion Is Irreversible

Deleted data **cannot be recovered**. Your meal history, progress, and profile will be gone permanently. If you later return to the App, you will need to create a brand-new account.

Questions? Email us at **tayabat.sys@gmail.com**.
