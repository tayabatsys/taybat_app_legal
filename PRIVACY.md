# Privacy Policy — AI-Taybat-App

**Effective date:** June 10, 2026
**Contact:** tayabat.sys@gmail.com

AI-Taybat-App ("the App", "we", "us") is a meal and nutrition application with Arabic language support that helps users follow a structured dietary system. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information needed to provide and improve the App:

- **Account information:** your email address, display name, and authentication identifiers when you sign up with email or sign in through a social provider.
- **Profile information:** optional details you provide such as gender, birth date, weight, height, and activity level, used to personalize your plan.
- **Health and dietary information:** dietary restrictions, health goals, health conditions you choose to share, meals you log, meal preferences, and weekly progress ratings.
- **Usage data:** basic interaction data such as feature usage and error reports, used to keep the App reliable.
- **Location (only if enabled):** approximate location, used solely for region-appropriate content. Location access is optional and off by default.

We do not sell your personal data to anyone.

## 2. How We Use Your Data

- To create and manage your account.
- To personalize your meal plan, recipe and meal recommendations, and progress tracking.
- To operate community features you choose to participate in.
- To analyze aggregate, anonymized usage so we can improve features and fix problems.
- To communicate important account or service updates.

## 3. Where Your Data Is Stored

Your data is stored in **Supabase (PostgreSQL)** infrastructure. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. Access to production data is restricted to authorized maintainers and protected by row-level security policies.

## 4. Third-Party Services

The App uses a small number of trusted third-party services:

- **Meta (Facebook Login):** if you sign in with Facebook, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Meta. We never receive your Facebook password.
- **Google (Sign-In and optional integrations):** if you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture. We never receive your Google password.
- **Supabase:** authentication, database, and file storage.

Each provider processes data under its own privacy policy. We only request the minimum permissions required for sign-in.

## 5. Your Rights

You can, at any time:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you.
- **Correct** inaccurate profile information directly in the App.
- **Delete** your account and associated data — see our [Data Deletion Policy](DELETION.md) or email us.

To exercise any right, contact **tayabat.sys@gmail.com**. We respond within 30 days.

## 6. Cookies and Tracking

The mobile App does not use advertising cookies and does not track you across other apps or websites. We use locally stored session tokens to keep you signed in, and minimal anonymized analytics to understand feature usage. No third-party advertising SDKs are included.

## 7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced in the App, and the effective date above will be updated.

## 9. Contact

For any privacy question or request: **tayabat.sys@gmail.com**
